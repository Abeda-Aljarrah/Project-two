Article Research

Dark matter and Dark energy

Click here to view the research
Abstract: in this article I present a review about dark matter and dark energy, starting with a brief history of the evidence of dark matter and its problem, then I introduce the modern evidence of its existence and the hypotheses about what dark matter could consist of. After that, I present the problem of dark energy, the essential things about it, and how we infer its existence. Finally, I introduce an alternative model of dark matter and dark energy and some additional information. This subject is one of the biggest unsolved problems in physics until now, where the nature of dark matter and dark energy, which is supposed to constitute about 95 percent of the energy density of the universe, is still a mystery.
